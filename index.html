Welcome !

Stuff the Bus is a three-week-long community-based school supply donation drive coordinated by Caribbean Alliance Insurance, to benefit students in need across the Cayman Islands.


In 2017, the initiative secured 14,490 school supply items. In 2018, we secured another 12,384 supplies and were able to distribute supplies to children in both Grand Cayman and Cayman Brac through our charity partners.


Then in 2019, over 27,000 school supplies were donated and $4,000 in pledges were made that were donated directly to the Cayman Islands Department of Education and used to purchase computer tablets for classrooms.
